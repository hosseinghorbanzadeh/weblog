About Me

My name is Hossein Ghorbanzadeh. I hold a Ph.D. in Software Engineering and currently work in the petrochemical industry.

An overview of Dr. Hossein Ghorbanzadeh’s academic background, expertise, and professional journey.

I was born in 1982 in Andimeshk, where I also completed my high school education. Between 2001 and 2021, I pursued and successfully completed my higher education from Bachelor's to Ph.D. in Software Engineering.

I am currently working in the petrochemical industry as a Senior Expert in Mechanized Systems. At present, I serve as the Project Manager for the implementation of an industrial ERP system.

Professional Experience and Expertise

  • Deployment of various enterprise software modules
  • Python programming
  • Django framework development

I specialize in the development and implementation of industrial ERP systems, with a strong focus on integrating IT and operational technologies (OT) in complex industrial environments.
